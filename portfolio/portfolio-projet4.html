TIFAOUINE CARAVAN - DEOUAR IMINOUAREG AZILAL

Project details

Description
I had the opportunity to participate in the Tifawine humanitarian caravan in Deouar Iminouarg as a design and media manager at the JLM Club, which is a mountainous village and rural commune in the Azilal province of the Tadla-Azilal region of Morocco; where 90 families were able to benefit from food baskets, blankets, warm clothes, renovation of a Msid, a mosque and an elementary school, thanks to the participation of various donors and several stakeholders who believed in this young and ambitious team known for its strong commitment and dedication of its members.

🔴Iminouar Ait Tamlil rural commune, a village located near Azilal, was a challenging place to reach, with extreme cold and remote location, but it was also a place of warmth, kindness, and generosity. The people we met there were simply amazing, and they welcomed us with open arms, making us feel at home.

🟡 I felt like I was with a new family, and everything seemed calm and easy for the four days I spent with everyone. The teamwork during the caravan was truly exceptional. The team renovated a school and two mosques, and distributed more then 80 food baskets to families in need, and i was more than happy to help with what i could do in renovating the school. It was an incredibly fulfilling experience to see the joy and gratitude on the faces of those we were able to help.

🟢 This was truly an experience of a lifetime, and I am proud to have been a part of it. I want to thank everyone involved for allowing me to be a part of this incredible caravan, and for reminding me of the true meaning of humanity. Let's continue to change the world, one caravan at a time!

Objectives
We all have fun observing the beauty of the landscapes of the great atlas, marvellous waterfalls and snow-covered mountain covered in snow. Unfortunately, behind these panoramic views live poor families suffering from difficult living conditions. In the absence of any means of transport, children take on the full risk of a daily challenge to get to elementary school. As young Moroccan men and women, we feel responsible for these innocent families and children. That's why the members of the JLM ENSET club organised the humanitarian caravan which taked place in IMINOUERG region AIT TIMLIL province of AZILLAL. The caravan aims to :

- Improve educational conditions.
- Reach out to local people with bags of food and clothing.
- Contribute to the sustainability of Koranic schools.
- Make young people aware of the importance of volunteer work.
