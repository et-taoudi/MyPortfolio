RBE7 SKILLS - YOUTH FOR "IMPACT"

Project details

Description
As a JLMist at JLM ENSET Mohammedia, AIESEC Mohammedia and AISEC Maroc, I was part of the organizing committee for one of the best opportunities for students in our community, RBE7 SKILLS as part of Global Entrepreneurship and Innovation Week, in its 2023 edition, held on March 15, 16 and 17.

+ RBE7 SKILLS is a competition and conference on social entrepreneurship for young people by young people. The competition aims to develop the leadership and skills of our young people. And this was only possible thanks to the support and participation of our conference speakers, industry professionals and passionate participants from different backgrounds.

+ It was an opportunity to further develop my project idea, so I participated with my teammate Abdessamad NTHELA by presenting our project called #SmartBin, an innovative solution that helps end-users supervise their dustbins remotely and enables them to receive alerts when the garbage can overflows.

+ The waste collection process is a critical aspect for the service providers. The traditional way of manually monitoring the wastes in waste bins is a complex, cumbersome process and utilizes more human effort, time and cost which is not compatible with the present day technologies.

+ Irregular management of waste typically domestic waste, industrial waste and environmental waste is a root cause for many of the human problems such as pollution, diseases and has adverse effects on the hygiene of living beings. In order to overcome all these problems, we are proposing the idea of the smart waste management system which helps in auto-management of waste without human interaction in order to maintain a clean environment.Thanks to our hard work and passion for innovation, our project qualified for the final.
