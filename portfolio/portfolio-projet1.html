LET'S ADOPT ECO-ACTIONS TO PROTECT OUR ENVIRONMENT!

Ui Design, Graphic

Project details

Description
Organization of a waste collection campaign in the public places of the city of Mohammedia. Contribution to the conceptual phase of the SMART FARM project initiated at our institution.

+ Action "0 waste": Awareness campaign to collect waste in public places in the city of Mohammedia in collaboration with #SOSNDD.
- March 25, 2022: Sablettes Beach
- March 26, 2022: Moulay Hassan Park, Center Beach, Place de la Marche Verte (Kasbah)
- March 27, 2022: Garden November 18, Abderrahim Bouabid Theater

+ SMART FARM: Contribution to the conceptual phase of the SMART FARM project initiated at our institution noting:
- The tracing of the ground SMART FARM
- The design of the 3D sketch of the smart farm
- The catalog of plants for the SMART FARM field, which includes the best plants that
absorb the humidity of Mohammedia and are the best adapted.

+ Publication of an article in professional journals on the occasion of the International Day of Biodiversity and Climate Change on May 22, in order to give a real and palpable image to young people about the need to contribute to the preservation of our ecosystem.

+ Organization of a conference under the theme: "Climate emergency and lucid hopes: AI in the service of climate change", animated by Mrs. TANHARTE Meriem.
