PHOTOGRAPHY CONTEST - PRECIOUS WATER

Project details

Description
The Multimedia Club organized a captivating photography competition entitled "Precious Water" as part of Global Entrepreneurship and Innovation week, which ran from March 15 to 17, 2023. The aim of the competition was to stimulate the dynamism and creativity of students in the field of photography, while raising public awareness of the importance of water as a rare and precious resource.

+ Water is a vital resource that plays an essential role in our daily lives, our environment and our economy. However, it is often taken for granted, and the "Precious Water" photography competition seeks to draw attention to its true value. Participants will be encouraged to explore different aspects of water, from its responsible use and conservation to its natural beauty and impact on society. They were invited to demonstrate their artistic talent by capturing images that illustrate the beauty, diversity and importance of water, experimenting with different styles of photography, from waterscapes to portraits highlighting water's interaction with people.

+ The selection of photographs was made by Mr. Rafik Mohammed, the patron of the Multimedia Club, and Mr. Habouch Hamid, director and head of operations at 2M. Their expertise and experience in the field of photography and media was invaluable in ensuring a fair and impartial selection of the best works.

+ A total of 13 talented participants submitted their stunning photos for the competition. Each brought his or her own artistic vision and creativity to capture the essence of precious water in its various forms and uses.
